The Best Movies According to Ken

My Top 5 Favourite Movies of All Time

In Time (2011)

An American science fiction action film depicting future world where money no longer exist.
Instead, people use time from their lifespans as primary currency.

The Pursuit of Happyness (2006)

An American biographical drama film about Chris Gardner, a homeless salesman, who struggled for his family's better life.

Avatar: The Way of Water (2023)

Jake Sully and Ney'tiri have formed a family and are doing everything to stay together. However, they must leave their home and explore the regions of Pandora.
When an ancient threat resurfaces, Jake must fight a difficult war against the humans.
